A.M. Paradox

Christopher Brent Wood, better known by his satge name Brent Faiyaz, is and American singer and record producer. He released his debut project, an extended play entitled A.M. Paradox, in 2016.

Brent Faiyaz, So Far Gone

Faiyaz began uploading his music onto SoundCloud in 2014 and moved from his hometown Columbia, Maryland to Charlotte, North Carolina before ultimately settling in Los Angeles, California to further his music career. On January 19, 2015, he released his debut single "Allure". On June 1, 2016, Faiyaz released "Invite Me", the lead single from his upcoming debut EP. On September 19, 2016, his 21st birthday, the EP entitled A.M. Paradox was released and had received positive reviews from music critics.
